Badges

Badges are emblematic awards you can receive when you meet certain criteria or achieve events. Once received, these badges are publicly show-cased on your profile page to show your accomplishments and skills. See: hackerone.com/[hacker-name]/badges.

All badges are represented by different images of bugs. Some badges have many levels depending on different criteria. These levels are represented by stars on the badge.

When you receive a badge for your profile page, each badge is composed of:

  • The name of the badge
  • A short description of what the badge is about
  • The date you achieved the badge

The following lists all of the current badges with a description of how to receive them:

Badge Description
Badger Made a badge suggestion that was implemented by HackerOne
Belle Of The Ball Submitted the top ranked report on the Hacktivity feed
Bounty Hunter 1, 10, or 100 bounties received.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive this badge whenever you receive 1, 10, and 100 bounties.
Champ Received when you get 1st, 2nd, or 3rd place on a quarterly leaderboard
Coinage Submitted a valid report during a HackerOne hackathon
Cryptolestes Solved a HackerOne Capture The Flag challenge
Diversity Reported bugs to 5, 20, or 50 different teams.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive a level of this badge whenever you report bugs to 5, 20, and 50 different teams.
Good Samaritan Resolved a report with a team that doesn't pay bounties
Greybeard Submitted valid reports in 3, 6, or 12 months in a row.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive a level of this badge whenever you submit valid reports in 3, 6, and 12 months in a row.
Hack The Box Bug Bounty Hunter Connect your HackerOne profile with Hack The Box using your student ID and complete the Bug Bounty Hunter path on Hack The Box
Hack The World Badass Reported 10, 20, or 50 valid issues during Hack the World.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive a level of this badge whenever you submit 10, 20, and 50 valid reports.
Hacking Hackers Hacked HackerOne
Happy Feat Suggested a feature that was implemented on HackerOne
Insecticide 1, 50, or 500 reports closed as resolved.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive a level of this badge when 1, 50, and 500 reports are closed as resolved.
On Fire Reported 10, 15, or 20 valid issues in a row during Hack the World.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive this badge whenever you report 10, 15, or 20 valid issues in a row.
Patient Waited more than six months for a report to get resolved
Publish Or Perish Publicly disclosed a report
Skills Got 1, 3, and 5 or more verified skills.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive a level of this badge when you have 1, 3, and 5 or more verified skills.
Spooky Submitted a valid report to the DoD
Streaker Received when 2, 5, or 10 reports in a row were closed as resolved.
There are multiple stages of this badge. You receive this badge when 2, 5, or 10 reports are closed or resolved in a row.
Swagger Received swag for the first time
Trailblazer Was the first of multiple reporters to report a vulnerability

Keep in mind that some badges are event-based.
